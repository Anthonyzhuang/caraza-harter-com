How can I get help ?

Welcome to your first course in Data programming, CS 220 (Data Programming I). To help you succeed we have assembled a team of 15 TAs and 14 Peer-mentors. They will be available to help you throughout the course for answering your questions, addressing your concerns or offering advice about future classes.

We will be offering the following avenues of communication for you:
  • Online synchronous meeting spaces
    • Lab Hours
    • Office hours
    • Social hours
  • Online asynchronous forums
    • Piazza (Recommended)
    • Email (*Only for emergencies)

Lab Hours

Open to all sessions where TAs and peer-mentors will be present to take questions from you. This space is for conceptual questions and discussions about the project strategies. Breakout rooms will be provided for you if you want to have a private conversation with other students.

  • These will be slightly different from office hours - these are open Q&A sessions for all students

  • This space is for conceptual questions and discussions about the project strategies.

  • Please join the Lab hours using Canvas->BBCollaborate Ultra

  • Schedule of the lab hours posted the Lab hours schedule on Tools->Piazza->Lab Hours.

    Note: We will be adding a couple of hours for our students currently not in Madison

  • Please watch the Lab Hours tutorial video for more details on what to expect and how to attend.

Office Hours

One-on-One sessions where TAs and peer-mentors will be present to answer your questions. This space is for all kinds of queries, especially those related to debugging your programs. TAs and peer-mentors will be present in separate breakout rooms and all conversations with you will be private.

  • These are private sessions for students to meet with their TAs or peer-mentors for a 1-on-1 Q&As.

  • This space is for more detailed questions and discussions about the everything related to the course. Bring your debugging questions here.

  • The availability of TAs and Peer-mentors is posted Tools->Piazza->Office Hours

    The schedule will be updated weekly.

  • To allow for us to help you in a meaningful manner please submit a ticket below before joining the office hours

Social Hours

Session where you can meet with other students in the class. It is an open to all BBCollaborate sessions where only peer-mentors may drop-in. This space is for you to meet other students in the class and have conversations as you would in the campus hallway after class.

You can access the Social Hours using Canvas->BBCollaborate Ultra.

Piazza

Piazza is an online forum for asking and answering questions about course material. You can access the course piazza page via Tools->Piazza.

Since this class is mostly online will prefer to use Piazza to resolve most of your queries regarding projects, quizzes and course material.

Email

To contact us over email, send an email to your assigned TA (Resources->Who is my TA?) and copy the following email id: cs220-fall20-help@cs.wisc.edu

Subject Tags

Your email subject line needs to have the following format. This will help us respond back to you in more effective and prompt manner.

[ Regrade | Admin | Exam | Quiz | Project] [Quiz # | Exam #| Project # | Topic Name | lesson #] 5-6 word summary of the query

Lets say you wanted help related to the exam, then your email would look as follows: 

  To: assigned_ta@wisc.edu; cs220-fall20-help@cs.wisc.edu
  Sub: [Exam] [Exam-1] Can't access exam solutions
  Hi TA,
      I need help with ...
