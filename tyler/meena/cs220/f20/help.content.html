How can I get help ?

Welcome to your first course in Data programming, CS 220 (Data Programming I). To help you succeed we have assembled a team of 15 TAs and 14 Peermentors. They will be available to help you throughout the course for answering your questions, addressing your concerns or offering advice about future classes.

We will be offering the following avenues of communication for you:
  • Online synchronous meeting spaces
    • Lab Hours
    • Office hours
    • Social hours
  • Online asynchronous forums
    • Piazza (Recommended)
    • Email (*Only for emergencies)

Piazza

Lab Hours

Open to all sessions where TAs and peer mentors will be present to take questions from you. This space is for conceptual questions and discussions about the project strategies. Breakout rooms will be provided for you if you want to have a private conversation with other students.

We have posted the Lab hours on Piazza

Office Hours

One-on-One sessions where TAs and peer mentors will be present to answer your questions. This space is for all kinds of queries, especially those related to debugging your programs. TAs and Peer mentors will be present in separate breakout rooms and all conversations with you will be private.

The availability of TAs and Peermentors is posted here, and will be updated weekly.

Once your ticket has be submitted you can track your position in the queue via this dashboard. Once a staff member has been assigned to you you will be placed in the In-Progress list. You will have about 2 Mins from that point onwards to join the meeting link posted on the dashboard, or on piazza or via Canvas->BBCollaborate Ultra.

Please use the following form to submit a request to be assigned a TA/Peermentor to talk to:

Social Hours

Open to all sessions where only peer mentors may be present. This space is for you to meet up with other students in the class and have conversations as you would in the hallway or after class. We will create a regular meeting room that will be linked here.

Email

To contact us over email, send an email to your assigned TA (Resources->Who is my TA?) and copy the following email id: cs220-fall20-help@cs.wisc.edu Your email subject line needs to have the following format to help us respond in a timely manner.

Subject Tags
[Quiz | Exam | Project | Help | Regrade | Admin] [Quiz #| Exam #| Project # | Topic Name/ lesson #]
  5-6 word summary of the query

You will have a specific staff member assigned to you. Please CC them in the email to get a response.
